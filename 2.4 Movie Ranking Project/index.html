The Best Movies According to Apurv

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Swades

Movie starred Shah Rukh Khan, who worked in NASA as a Scientist and came to India. After coming to his Mother nation he realised country needs him.

Kho Gaye Hum Kahan

Movie based on Today's Social media Generation. A group of three friends, who are fighting with their own traumas and failer.

12th Fail

Movie based on a real life IAS Officer. The movie shows the entire journey of officer, how he had achieved his position in this difficult world.
